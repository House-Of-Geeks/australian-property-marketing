# Australian Property Marketing Website Specification

## Overview
A lead-generation website for Australian Property Marketing targeting independent real estate agency principals.

## Primary Goal
Generate qualified leads for a $5,000 marketing profitability audit.

## Secondary Goal
Enquiries for ongoing marketing services ($10,000+ per month retainers).

## Target Audience
- Principals and owners of independent real estate agencies
- Agencies with 10-50 agents
- $2M-$10M annual Gross Commission Income
- Already spending on marketing but frustrated with ROI visibility

## Brand Colors
- Primary (Coral): #F05C3E
- Charcoal: #1a1a1a
- Soft Grey: #f8f9fa
- White: #ffffff

## Typography
- Display: Instrument Serif
- Body: Outfit
